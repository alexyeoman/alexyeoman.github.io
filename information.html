Insider Treats

Most organizations focus their security efforts on external threats, perimeter security, and exterior-mounted surveillance cameras. However, the more meaningful and credible risk is from your own people inside those gates and walls. Information and data is more transitive, and new technology provides an avenue for protected content to be distributed in the flash of a keystroke. Consequently, an insider threat management program should be comprehensive, including pre-employment background checks, orientation training for new employees, data transfer restrictions, tagging of key assets, and a well-defined employee termination protocol. BGI can identify gaps in your security and create a balanced strategy to protect your future.

Our Services in Insider Treats Include:

  • Pre and Post-Employment Background Checks
  • Visitors Policies
  • Computer and Cyber Precautions
  • Simulations, Drills, and Tabletops
  • Employee Awareness and Education Training: An informed and empowered employee is your best defense.
