Our Vision at BGI

BGI was founded in 2014 to provide global strategic solutions with an emphasis on individualized service.

The security landscape has evolved greatly in recent years due to several pivotal events in our country’s history which have illuminated the breadth and depth of corporate security needs. The terrorist events of 9-11, Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans, and the Enron accounting scandal – among others – have created a need for broader security and safety measures.

Conventional security policy based on fences, cameras, and guards are no longer suitable for such an environment. This expanding set of risks, coupled with emerging technology, pose new challenges for corporate security directors saddled with keeping companies and employees safe. As security challenges become broader and more diverse, policies must be crafted to address contemporary challenges in this dynamic environment. Traditional approaches will no longer be effective.

If security becomes inherent in the corporate culture, then there will be not only a greater focus on risk analysis, but also on opportunities to scale back security. The strategic reduction or elimination of ineffective policies can allow for new business models – that is, a strategy to achieve long-term financial strength as a company. This new corporate philosophy of security must emphasize legitimate risks and opportunities.

While security challenges often overlap sectors and industries, solutions must be customized and thoughtfully crafted to best address those challenges. A successful program launched at one company might be a dismal failure at another. BGI assesses these nuances in creating strategies that balance risks and threats with global market standards and a best practices philosophy. All solutions are enterprise based, scalable, and affordable.
